About the Series

Bleach is a manga and anime series created by Tite Kubo. It follows Ichigo Kurosaki, a high school student who gains the powers of a Soul Reaper — a being tasked with defending humans from evil spirits and guiding lost souls to the afterlife.

Story Overview

After accidentally obtaining Soul Reaper abilities from Rukia Kuchiki, Ichigo is drawn into the supernatural world of Hollows, Soul Society, and powerful spiritual battles. As the series progresses, he discovers hidden truths about his heritage and faces increasingly dangerous enemies.

Main Themes

  • Responsibility and Duty
  • Identity and Self-Discovery
  • Sacrifice and Protection
  • Strength Through Bonds

Why It Is Part of the Big 3

Bleach gained massive popularity during the 2000s and became known for its stylish character designs, intense sword battles, and memorable soundtrack. Its strong presence in Weekly Shōnen Jump and international television broadcasts secured its place in the legendary Old Big 3.
