About the Series

Naruto is a Japanese manga and anime series created by Masashi Kishimoto. The story follows Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja from the Hidden Leaf Village who dreams of becoming the Hokage — the strongest ninja and leader of his village.

Story Overview

Naruto was born with the Nine-Tailed Fox sealed inside him, which caused the villagers to fear and isolate him. Despite this, he grows up determined to earn respect and prove his worth. Throughout the series, he forms strong bonds with teammates Sasuke Uchiha and Sakura Haruno while facing powerful enemies and uncovering deep ninja secrets.

Main Themes

  • Perseverance and Hard Work
  • Friendship and Loyalty
  • Redemption and Forgiveness
  • Believing in Yourself

Why It Is Part of the Big 3

Naruto became one of the most influential anime of the 2000s, helping introduce Japanese animation to a global audience. Its long serialization in Weekly Shōnen Jump and strong character development solidified its place among the legendary Old Big 3.
